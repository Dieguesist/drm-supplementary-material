# Expert Evaluation Interview Guide

Semi-structured interview protocol used in the formative evaluation of the Digital Risk Metamodel (V1). Each session followed three phases. The artifact and its objectives were presented to the participant before Phase 2.

## Phase 1: Demographic characterization

1. What is your professional position?
2. How many years of experience do you have in the area?
3. Do you have any certifications?
4. What is your familiarity with the ArchiMate language? (5-point Likert scale, where 1 indicates no familiarity and 5 indicates expert-level proficiency)

## Phase 2: Structured assessment

Participants answered four closed questions on a 5-point Likert scale: 1 (strongly disagree), 2 (disagree), 3 (neither agreement nor disagreement), 4 (agree), 5 (strongly agree).

1. What is the level of agreement with the structure of the model?
2. What is the level of agreement with the objective of the artifact?
3. What is the agreement with the completeness of domain concepts present in the model?
4. What is the level of agreement with the adequacy of the model towards the practice of Digital Risk Management?

## Phase 3: Qualitative feedback

Participants were invited to provide open-ended feedback and criticisms, and to elaborate on the reasoning behind their scoring decisions, including perceived strengths, limitations, and opportunities for improvement of the artifact.

---

Participation was voluntary and results are reported in aggregated form, consistent with the conditions of participant consent.
